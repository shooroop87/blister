How It Works

How Does BlisterPerPost Work?

Managing daily medication can be time-consuming and confusing. With BlisterPerPost, your medication is safely pre-sorted by dose and time of day — delivered to your home every 14 days. Stay safe, organized, and stress-free.

Request Your Prescription

Ask your doctor for a prescription and a copy of your medication plan to start your personalized blister service.

Complete the Registration Form

Download our BlisterPerPost Registration Form (PDF) and the Prepaid Envelope (PDF). If you prefer, we can also send both directly to your home — just click here: Request Information Material.

Enjoy Your BlisterPerPost Service

Receive your individual blister rolls every 14 days – pre-sorted, clearly labeled, and ready for use. Your first delivery includes a free blister box for convenient storage.
