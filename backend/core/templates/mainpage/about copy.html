About Us

Simplifying Daily Medication with Precision, Safety and Care

At BlisterPerPost, we help patients, families, and care facilities organize medications safely and effortlessly — one blister at a time.

BlisterPerPost was founded to make medication management safer, simpler, and more reliable for everyone who depends on daily treatment. Our team of pharmacists uses state-of-the-art blister technology to pre-sort each tablet by time and dosage — reducing confusion, preventing errors, and giving patients peace of mind.

We work closely with doctors, caregivers, and healthcare providers across Germany and Austria to ensure that every blister is checked, verified, and delivered on time — directly to your home or care facility.

Behind every pack is our commitment to pharmaceutical precision and human care. With a focus on safety, data protection, and reliability, we help thousands of patients take the right medication at the right time — every single day.
